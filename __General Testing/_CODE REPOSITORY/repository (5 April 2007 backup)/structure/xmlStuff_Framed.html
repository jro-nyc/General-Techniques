  1. Your domain should reflect your business name, products you offer, or topics your site will cover.

  2. Use only letters, numbers, and hyphens (spaces and other symbols aren't allowed).

  3. Try .net and .org extensions — they're as well-known as .com, but you'll have more options.

  4. If your first choice isn't available, try rearranging the word order or using abbreviations.

  5. For variety, add hyphens or words such as online, store, the, or shop to your business name.
