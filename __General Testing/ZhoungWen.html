Matching 

Practical Chinese Reader 1-5
Practice the 30 vocabulary words from Practical Chinese Reader chapters 1-5. For extra practice, try writing the characters on a separate sheet of paper as you play the games. In these games, tones are represented as follows:

tone 1 (flat): mä
tone 2 (rising): má
tone 3 (falling, rising): mâ
tone 4 (falling): mà
     

Directions: Find the matching squares.
